Sonic The Hedgehog

Humble Beginings

Sonic the Hedgehog is a gaming character that many players know and love. He was first introduced in 1991 on the Sega Genisis. The role you would play is a Blue hegehog named Sonic and you would race and clear courses while battling robots. The robot were those of Doctor Ivo "Eggman" Robotnink the main enemy of Sonic. Your job was to stop Robotnink from taking over the world by racing through stages like the Green Hill Zone.

Becoming Popular

In 1992 A game called Sonic the Hedgehog 2 came out increasing its popularity. I introduced a new companion named Miles "Tails" Prower, who would travel along-side you and help you beat Doctor Robotnink.
